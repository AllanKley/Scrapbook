First tracked sync since the vault reorganization. `criacao-de-personagem`
mechanics (atributos, classe, domínio, patrono, perícias, conexões,
equipamentos and its subpages) now live nested under
`mecanicas/criacao de personagem/`, and the world-lore pages (lore_mundo,
geografia mundial, ecos, comércio, rank) moved into their own
`world_building` section — both show up as their own groups on the
[Dungeon Rift](#/devlog) index now.

**New pages**

- [Progressão de Personagem](#/devlog/entry/progressao-de-personagem) — how
  Rank works mechanically: the milestone-based ladder, the Rank Test for
  social recognition, and what a character gains at each step.
- [Rank](#/devlog/entry/rank) — what Rank means in the world (the Hunters'
  Association, the gap between real power and official recognition).

**Rules changes**

- Domínio abilities now progress on **Rank E/C/A/S**, distinct from Classe's
  **F/D/B/S** — the two ladders interleave instead of sharing rank letters.
- Atributos and Perícias both gained a "Crescimento pós-criação" section:
  attributes can climb past the creation-time cap of 4 (up to 7) and skills
  can climb past Elite's usual limits, both via Patrono milestone ranks.

**Removed**

- Dropped the Homebrewery rulebook export and the TODO note — both moved to
  a private folder in the vault and are no longer meant to be public.

Plus smaller wording and cross-link fixes across `comece-aqui`,
`criacao-de-personagem`, `patrono`, `geografia-mundial`, `lore-mundo`,
`reliquias`, `infusoes` and `itens-gerais`.
