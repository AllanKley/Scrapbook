A big terminology and rules pass across `criacao de personagem` and combat.
Atributos are now **Traços**, Classe is now **Linhagem**, and the old "Core"
that a Fratura protects is now an **Âncora** — the old pages moved to
[traços](#/devlog/entry/tracos) and [linhagem](#/devlog/entry/linhagem), and
`atributos`/`classe` were removed from the devlog since their content lives
under the new slugs.

**New pages**

- [Recursos](#/devlog/entry/recursos) — the derived-stats page: PV, PA, RD,
  Defesa e Evasão, Deslocamento and Subespaço, each pointing back to the
  mechanic that produces it.
- [Benefícios Sociais](#/devlog/entry/beneficios-sociais) — Padrão de Vida,
  the wealth-tier ladder (Modesto through Milionário) that replaces
  tracking day-to-day money.

**Rules changes**

- **Pontos de Essência (PE) removed.** Domínio and Linhagem abilities that
  used to scale with PE spent now cost PA only, usually gated by a
  once-per-scene or once-per-rest limit instead — see the "Redesenhada sem
  PE" notes scattered across [domínio](#/devlog/entry/dominio).
- **Attacking now costs 2 PA** (was 1), and **passive Defense is gone** — an
  attack auto-hits unless the target spends a reaction to contest it via
  [Defender](#/devlog/entry/tipos-de-acoes) (shield users) or
  [Esquivar](#/devlog/entry/tipos-de-acoes) (everyone else, now testing the
  new Evasão skill instead of Reflexo).
- **Multiclasse de Domínio** — characters can now invest Domínio levels into
  a second Domínio instead of only deepening their starting one; see
  [domínio](#/devlog/entry/dominio) for how PV and Deslocamento are and
  aren't shared across trilhas.
- PV and Deslocamento are now per-Domínio (shared by both specializations)
  rather than per-specialization.
- Pontos de Superação were cut; helping on a Planned Action now grants
  Fortuna directly instead of going through a points pool.
- A draft exploding-damage-die crit rule was added to
  [tipos de ações](#/devlog/entry/tipos-de-acoes), flagged as untested at
  the table.

Plus consistency edits for the Traços/Linhagem/Vínculos/Arsenal renames and
wording fixes across `testes-de-pericias`, `progressao-de-personagem`,
`patrono`, `pericias`, `comercio`, `geografia-mundial` and most Linhagem
pages.
